Composition plan: song 1 (140.1 s, 27 cycles)

Overall arc

  1. opening — ground in shadow [cycles 0–2]

    Establish stillness on a near-tonic A. A single solitary anchor in deep shadow — moonlight as a thin line, or a head bowed. Leave generous space; the room is just beginning to listen.

  2. first ascent — warmth gathers [cycles 3–7]

    Centroid rises and pitch shifts to C# / G#. The room warms: an open palm finding low candlelight, loose linen catching soft side light. Begin gentle motion on one anchor; do not crowd.

  3. muhayyar gesture — the high D crossing [cycles 8–10]

    Cycle 8 brings the only D of the track at the brightest centroid — the closest the piece gets to a muhayyar arrival. Commit with elevation, not impact: a soft bloom, a sustained line of moonlight widening, a luminous photographic anchor. Background may shift toward pale candlelight.

  4. sustained middle — density without rupture [cycles 11–15]

    Dense articulation across F#, G#, C#, B. Vary earlier motifs rather than invent. A single feather might tremble; loose linen might drift; the back of someone at a window joins the room. One composite scene lives here.

  5. phrase break and quiet descent [cycles 16–20]

    Cycle 16 is a deep exhale — dark timbre, very sparse, low centroid. Fade aging material. Allow stillness, then drift through quiet falling cycles with breath on cold glass and a solitary figure receding into dusk.

  6. late rising — a second gathering [cycles 21–23]

    Centroid lifts again, articulation thickens. Echo an earlier motif (the line of moonlight, or the open palm) in a new position or weight. Compositional memory, not new invention.

  7. final tableau — settling [cycles 24–26]

    Stop opening new threads. Hold the strongest image/text/SVG relationship. The last cycle rises slightly — let one breath release, do not punctuate. End with the room composed: a thin line of moonlight, an open palm, one phrase of testimony.

Element vocabulary

Anchors: thin line of moonlight on still water at night, open palm in low candlelight, head bowed in shadow with soft side light, single hand cupped around a small flame, loose linen falling across a dark surface, single feather on dark cloth, the back of a person looking out a window at night, solitary figure walking across a dark plain at dusk, breath on cold glass at night, slow ripple expanding on a dark pool
